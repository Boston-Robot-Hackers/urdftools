
A diff drive robot with two wheels and a caster
It has a base that is circular, 3mm thick and 15cm in radius
The wheels are each 7cm diameter and 2cm wide
The wheels are 10cm apart and forward 10cm from the center
The caster is centered in the back and 12cm back from the center
The lidar is centerd on the base and 10cm high. The lidar has a square post holding up.
The lidar itself is a cylinder 3cm radius and 5cm tall
The imu is 3cm forward of the center and 5cm above the base
the imu is oriented with x facing back and z out the left side of the robot

build a durdf yaml file for this robot
Define all the constants in front.
Call it dome2.durdf
